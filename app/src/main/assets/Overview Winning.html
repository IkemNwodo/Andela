At Andela, we know that effective communication is required for use to succeed so we ask questions, request and give feedback, write professionally, speak to be understood and seek to understand the message being passed. We act, react and communicate in a manner that demonstrates respect for our coworkers, partners, ecosystem. We show an acceptance of the organization's mission and hold ourselves to the highest ethical standard

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to understand:

  1. Principles of effective communication

  2. Importance and benefits of seeking feedback

  3. Approaches for constructively and effectively receiving and giving feedback

  4. Vital rules of business etiquette

  5. What ethics is

  6. Project management tools
